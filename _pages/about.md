Welcome! I am **Tan-Phuc Nguyen**, a PhD student in Mathematics at **Texas A&M University**, supervised by **Prof. Minh-Binh Tran**. Before starting my PhD, I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics Teacher Education from Ho Chi Minh City University of Education, where I completed my undergraduate thesis on regularity of PDEs  under the supervision of Prof. Thanh-Nhan Nguyen. My current research lies at the intersection of **partial differential equations (PDEs)**, **control theory**, and **numerical analysis**. I am particularly interested in the analysis and numerical approximation of PDE control problems, as well as their applications in scientific computing.

## Research Interests

- Partial Differential Equations
- Control Theory
- Numerical Analysis
- Scientific Computing
