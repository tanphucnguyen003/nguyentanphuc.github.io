Welcome! I am  <b style="color: darkred;">Tan-Phuc Nguyen</b>, a PhD student at the [Department of Mathematics](https://artsci.tamu.edu/mathematics/index.html), [<span style="color: green;">Texas A&M University</span>](https://www.tamu.edu/), supervised by [<span style="color: darkred;">Prof. Minh-Binh Tran</span>](https://minhbinhtran.org/). Before starting my PhD, I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics Teacher Education from [<span style="color: green;">Ho Chi Minh City University of Education</span>](https://hcmue.edu.vn/en/), where I completed my undergraduate thesis on regularity of PDEs under the supervision of [<span style="color: darkred;">Prof. Thanh-Nhan Nguyen</span>](https://sites.google.com/site/nguyenthnhan/home). My current research lies at the intersection of partial differential equations (PDEs), control theory, and numerical analysis.
